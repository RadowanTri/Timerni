We Are Always Waiting For You

Time research & innovation is a research organisation based in the United Kingdom and Bangladesh. We have started various research in behavioural computer sciences; technology, health informatics, education and community. Our supreme emphasis is to execute research, training and development activities initially in the UK and Bangladesh to help disadvantaged people.
